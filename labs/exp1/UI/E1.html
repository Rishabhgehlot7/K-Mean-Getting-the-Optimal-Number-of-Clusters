Instructions

  1. Enter the values of x and y in the data set and Click on the ADD button.
  2. Repeat step 1 up to 8 times.
  3. Click on the NEXT button.
  4. Select centroids and click on the SUBMIT button.
  5. Find out the Euclidean distance between a centroid and a data point by clicking on the DISTSNCE button.
  6. Click on the CLUSTER button to find cluster of each data point.
  7. Click on the CENTROIDS button and compute the new centroid of each Cluster.
  8. Repeat steps 4 to 7 until cluster value and new clsuter value are not matched.
  9. If the Cluster matches the new Cluster, then click on the NEXT button and enter the required value in the SSE equation.
  10. After finding total SSE click on the NEXT button and repeat steps 8 to 9 for K = 2 to 7.
  11. Click on the PLOT button to show the graph.
