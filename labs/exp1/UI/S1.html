Instructions

  1. Enter the values of x and y in the data set and Click on the ADD button.
  2. Repeat step 1 up to 5 times.
  3. Click on the NEXT button.
  4. Select centroids and click on the SUBMIT button.
  5. Find out the Euclidean distance between a centroid and a data point by clicking on the DISTANCE button.
  6. Click on the CLUSTER button to find cluster of each data point.
  7. Click on the CENTROIDS button and compute the new centroid of each Cluster.
  8. Repeat steps 5 to 7 until cluster value and new clsuter value are not matched.
  9. If the Cluster matches the New Cluster, then click on the NEXT button and then click on the COHESION.
  10. Click on the SEPARATION button.
  11. After put the COHESION and SEPARATION in Silhouette coefficient equation from table and Click on the SUBMIT button.
  12. Put the all Silhouette score in Silhouette coefficient equation from table for finding average Silhouette score.
  13. Click on the NEXT button.
  14. Repeat Step 8 to 14 for K = 3, 4, 5.
  15. Click on the PLOT button to show the graph.
